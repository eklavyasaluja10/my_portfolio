SERIES NO. 04 — MIDNIGHT CHRONICLES

Urban Silence

The city breathes only when we stop listening.

'Urban Silence' is a meditation on the architectural solitude found within the world's most densely populated hubs. By capturing these spaces during the "blue hour," the series strips away the chaos of commerce and movement.

Each frame serves as a vacuum; preserving a singular moment where the concrete and steel seem to find their own pulse. It is an exploration of the quietude that remains when the human element is momentarily absent.

"Movement is the illusion of life."

I chase the moments that exist between heartbeats—the quiet gravity of a gaze, the way light bends through an unfamiliar city, the stillness that hides inside chaos.

Based in India but drawn to every corner of the world, my photography is a dialogue between presence and absence. I believe every frame is a ghost of a reality that will never repeat itself— a single breath held still against the passage of time.

The collections here are not mere galleries—they are composed sequences, each image chosen for the emotional residue it leaves behind. My lens does not simply document; it listens, and then it speaks.
